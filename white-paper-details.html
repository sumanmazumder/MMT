Overview

These days, businesses try to use all online channels to promote their business and reach a wide audience. Apart from website, mobile apps and progressive web apps are two popular platforms for online marketing. However, many business owners get confused when they need to choose between these two options. Both native apps and PWAs have unique benefits. It’s important to understand the company requirements. If one’s business needs can be satisfied with the features offered by native apps, then native apps should be preferred; if the requirements of a business are met by PWAs, then these are the best bet for that business.

  • Definition of native apps

  • Benefits of native apps

  • Definition of PWAs

  • Benefits of PWAs

  • Differences between native apps and PWAs
